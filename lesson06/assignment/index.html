Did you know 9 out of 10 car seats are misused!

If you want to beat these odds and maximize vehicle safety for your child, then this is for you! Come get one-on-one guidance on using your child car seat.

Bring your car seat, your car, and your child and be prepared to have hands-on training. Expectant parents and other caregivers also welcome.
